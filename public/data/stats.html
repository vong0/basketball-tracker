Shot tracking guide

Contest categories

  • Wide open: defender cannot affect the shot, usually 6+ feet away/no real closeout.
  • Open: defender nearby but late/not affecting, usually 3–6 feet.
  • Light: defender within about 1–3 feet, hand up/late, some effect but clean look.
  • Heavy: defender strongly affects shot: rushed, altered, body pressure.
  • Blocked/smothered: block/partial block or defender in shooting space.

Shot quality in reports

  • Open Shot Rate: wide open + open attempts. This is the share of clean looks.
  • Light Contest Shot Rate: light contest attempts. These are playable but not totally clean looks.
  • Bad Shot Rate: heavy + blocked/smothered attempts. This flags rushed or smothered attempts.

Shot types

  • Catch-and-shoot: catch into shot, including slow rhythm one-dribble if it does not create separation.
  • Pull-up: shot created off dribble, attack, multiple dribbles, or separation move.
  • Floater/runner: short touch shot in lane or paint, usually off movement.
  • Layup: rim attempt that is not a dunk or floater.
  • Putback: immediate shot after an offensive rebound.
  • Cut: shot created by off-ball cut to the basket.
  • Transition: early offense before defense is fully set.

Assists and screen context

  • Normal assists only go on made shot rows through assisted_by.
  • If a screen created the shot, fill screen_assist_by and screen_type on the shot row.
  • Also add a screen event if the screener deserves credit.

Report/stat guide

PTS

Points scored by player/team from made shots and free throws.

FG / FG%

Field goals made/attempted. FG% = FGM divided by FGA.

2PT / 3PT

Two-point and three-point makes/attempts.

FT / FT%

Free throws made/attempted and percentage.

eFG%

Effective FG%. Shooting efficiency that gives extra value to made threes.

TS%

True shooting. Overall scoring efficiency including free throws.

PTS/Poss

Points divided by estimated possessions used.

Poss Used

Raw estimated scoring/turnover possessions used by a player or team.

Usage%

Player Poss Used divided by team Poss Used. It is a share, not a raw count.

AST

Normal assists from made shots. These come from assisted_by in Shots.

AST PTS

Points created by normal assists, usually 2 or 3 per assisted make.

AST/TO

Assists divided by turnovers. Higher is usually cleaner decision-making.

Extra Potential AST

Good passes creating immediate chances that did not become normal assists.

ADV

Advantage created: collapse defense, force rotation, advantage pass, or create an edge.

ADV PTS

Points directly coming from advantage-created events.

Paint Touch Created

Paint touch that bends the defense or creates a scoring chance.

Drive Kick Created

Drive-and-kick action that creates a shot advantage, usually a three.

Screen AST

Screen directly creates a made basket.

Screen Opportunity

Screen creates a good shot/foul/advantage but no immediate made basket.

Screen Created Total

Screen assists + screen opportunities + screen advantages.

OREB / DREB / REB

Offensive rebounds, defensive rebounds, and total rebounds.

Def Activity

Steals + blocks + deflections. A quick activity/pressure indicator.

FTr

Free throw rate. Free throw attempts divided by field goal attempts.

Open / Light / Bad%

Shot attempt distribution by contest quality.

Zone FG

FGM/FGA and FG% by court region.

Contest FG

FGM/FGA and FG% by contest level.

+/-

Team point differential while player/lineup was on court.

Off / Def / Net Rtg

Points for, points allowed, and net points per 100 possessions.

How to read the report

  • Team control dashboard: quick summary of shooting, shot quality, possession control, and glass/activity.
  • Creation leaders: who passed, bent the defense, and created extra shots.
  • Screening leaders: who created value without taking the shot.
  • Shot map: where shots came from and whether each zone was efficient.
  • Lineup report: which five-player groups won or lost their minutes.
